How to Play

1. Set Your Difficulty

Before you start, use the dropdown menu to choose how many pairs you want to remember (3, 4, or 5). This can only be set at the start of a session.

2. Observe the Sequence

The game displays pairs (e.g., A1). Each appears for 3s with a 2s break. Memorize them in the order they appear.

3. Enter Your Answer

Type the sequence into the box. You have 10s to submit.

4. Track Your Stats

Check the bottom of the screen for your Success Rate and a performance-based cat 😺 image!

🔄 Starting New: To change the number of pairs again or reset your statistics, please refresh the page (F5).

Good luck!
